Drop in and hot-desk in an open-plan workspace, or reserve your own dedicated desk in a shared office.

FAQs

How much does a coworking space in Ahmedabad costs?
Join a thriving community of professionals with our vibrant coworking spaces in Ahmedabad. Prices for renting a desk in a shared workspace start from INR 8,790 to INR 12,390 per person per month. These prices are based on Regus.com prices and a 24-month contract. The actual price will vary by number of people, term of agreement, the specific coworking location and availability.
Can I book a coworking desk for a day in Ahmedabad?
Yes, you can use a shared workspace in Ahmedabad for as long or as short as you need. Rent coworking space from as little as an hour to a day.
What types of workspace can you rent in Ahmedabad?
You can either rent a dedicated desk that's reserved just for you, or drop-in and hot desk on demand at our centres in Ahmedabad.
Do I need to book a coworking space in advance?
If you have reserved a dedicated desk in Ahmedabad, there's no need to book in advance. However, if you prefer to hot desk, it's a good idea to book a space via the Regus app, online or by phone to ensure a space is available.
Is there a minimum rental term?
There is no minimum rental term when using our coworking spaces. You can use a coworking space in any of our centres around the world for a minimum of one hour or reserve a dedicated desk for as long as you need. For even more flexibility, our coworking membership gives you access to coworking desks by the day or for five, ten or unlimited days' access per month.
